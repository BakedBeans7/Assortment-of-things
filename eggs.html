Scrambled Eggs and CHICKEN

Eggs and Chicken are some of my favorite foods. Heres is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs and the 11 KFC spices. I dont know what you need KFC spices for but there here if you need them.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (It will continue to cook on the plate until served)

THE KFC 11 SPICES (recipe was found by Sander's nephew from a scrapbook, it may not be the same one today)

  • 2/3 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon basil
  • 1/3 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried mustard
  • 4 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 3 teaspoons white pepper

The spices are mixed with 2 cups of flour to create the KFC breading.
